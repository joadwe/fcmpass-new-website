# Frequently Asked Questions

??? question "Is FCM<sub>PASS</sub> free?"
    FCM<sub>PASS</sub> is **free for academic users** at authorized, accredited, degree-granting institutions. If your use case involves commercial work, fee-for-service, or industrial collaboration, a paid commercial license is required. See our [pricing page](pricing.md) for details.

??? question "What cytometers are supported?"
    FCM<sub>PASS</sub> supports instruments from Agilent, Beckman Coulter, Waters (formerly Becton Dickinson), Cytek Biosciences, nanofcm, Sony Biosciences, and ThermoFisher Scientific. See the [full cytometer list](getting-started/cytometer-support.md) for specific models. If your cytometer is missing, email [info@fcmpass.com](mailto:info@fcmpass.com) with an example .fcs file and we'll add support.

??? question "What operating systems are supported?"
    - **Windows:** Windows 10 (21H2+) and Windows 11
    - **macOS:** macOS Monterey (12.6) through macOS Sonoma (14), Apple Silicon (M-series) only

    See [install requirements](getting-started/install-requirements.md) for full hardware and software specifications.

??? question "How do I install FCM<sub>PASS</sub> on macOS?"
    macOS may block the installer because it is not yet signed with an Apple Developer certificate. You can resolve this by running a single terminal command to remove the quarantine flag. See the [installation guide](getting-started/installation.md) for step-by-step instructions.

??? question "My institutional email address is not recognized. What do I do?"
    You can check whether your email domain is recognized at [check.fcmpass.com](https://check.fcmpass.com/){ target="_blank" }. If it is not listed, contact [info@fcmpass.com](mailto:info@fcmpass.com) and we will review your institution for inclusion.

??? question "How long does a license last?"
    All licenses — academic and commercial — are valid for **one year** from the date of activation and can be renewed. Demo licenses are valid for 30 days.

??? question "Can I use FCM<sub>PASS</sub> for commercial work?"
    Yes, with a **paid commercial license**. Academic (free) licenses may not be used for commercial R&D, fee-for-service work, or industrial collaborations where outputs are shared with commercial entities. Visit our [pricing page](pricing.md) or [get a quote](https://quote.fcmpass.com){ target="_blank" }.

??? question "How many computers can I install FCM<sub>PASS</sub> on?"
    Academic licenses allow installation on up to **2 computers**, provided the software is only used on one at a time. Paid licenses allow installation on **1 computer**, but can be deactivated and transferred to a different computer once per license term.

??? question "How do I get a quote for a commercial license?"
    You can generate a self-service quote instantly at [quote.fcmpass.com](https://quote.fcmpass.com){ target="_blank" }, or contact [sales@fcmpass.com](mailto:sales@fcmpass.com) directly.

??? question "Where can I manage my account and license?"
    Your account and license can be managed at [license.fcmpass.com](https://license.fcmpass.com){ target="_blank" } using your registered credentials.

??? question "I didn't receive my license key email. What should I do?"
    Check your **Junk/Spam** folder for an email from info@fcmpass.com. If it's not there, contact [info@fcmpass.com](mailto:info@fcmpass.com) for assistance.
