## Academic License

The software is available for **free** to users at authorized and accredited degree-granting institutions, provided the software is used solely for academic research purposes. Academic licenses **may not** be used for:

- Commercial business, including R&D, marketing, or sales literature
- Fee-for-service work performed for customers outside the licensee's institution
- Industrial collaborations where software outputs are shared with commercial entities

!!! tip "Check your eligibility"
    You can verify whether your institutional email address is recognized at [check.fcmpass.com](https://check.fcmpass.com/){ target="_blank" }. If your email is not recognized, contact [info@fcmpass.com](mailto:info@fcmpass.com).
